# Minion-Hub
Modern laptops are becoming increasingly slimmer and lighter, often at the expense of having fewer built-in USB ports. Therefore, a USB hub is essential for expanding connectivity and accomodating multiple USB devices simultaneously. However, there is one common problem to implement this device, that is EXPENSIVE. 
A standard industrialised USB hub costs around 20 USD onwards, but what if I tell you that you could build one for just less than 10 USD.

# How it works
- Plug in a USB type C cable to the bottom USB type C upstream port to power up the USB hub.
- Use any 4 USB ports on the side of the USB hub to power up your devices simultaneously.

# Why this and not other products
- Cost effective. A standard industrialised grade USB hub often costs around over 20 USD, however, over here you could just build one USB hub for just less than 10 USD and even have the option to include custom art or illustrations to the USB hub itself.
- Customizable. If you want other colours, you get other colours than yellow. If you want more USB ports you could just add more to it. If you don't like the positions of the USB ports are placed, change them.
- Portable. Industrialised USB hub often ignore the word "PORTABLE" and tends to solely focusing on performance. On the other hand, this USB hub has a built-in hole mainly for keychain rings to slip right through it and attach it to anywhere you want.
